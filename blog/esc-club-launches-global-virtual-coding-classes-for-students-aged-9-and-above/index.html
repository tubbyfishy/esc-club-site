ESC Club Launches Global Virtual Coding Classes for Students Aged 9 and Above

STEM · by alex · June 27, 2024

ESC Club is excited to announce a series of virtual online coding classes for students aged 9 and above, available worldwide during the summer break of 2024.

These innovative classes are designed to introduce young learners to the exciting world of coding without the need for difficult text-based languages.

Class Features

Multilingual Support:
The primary language of instruction is English. However, to ensure inclusivity and comprehension, each class is supplemented with slides and additional support in three languages: Spanish, Chinese, and French.

This multilingual approach helps break down language barriers, making coding accessible to a diverse group of students.

Live Tri-Language Teacher Assistants:
During each online session, live tri-lingual teacher assistants will be available to provide real-time support.

This ensures that students can receive immediate help and clarification in their preferred language, enhancing their learning experience and fostering a more inclusive environment.

Enrollment and Reach

The classes have garnered interest from students across various countries, including the United States, Mexico, Canada, and mainland China. This global participation highlights the widespread appeal and accessibility of the ESC Club’s coding curriculum.

Promotion and Outreach

Information about the classes has been widely shared across multiple social media platforms such as Facebook, YouTube, WeChat, and Redbook.
