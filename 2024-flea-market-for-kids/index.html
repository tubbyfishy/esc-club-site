2024 Flea Market for kids

By alex — April 18, 2025

Date: [12/07/24 ]

⏰ Time: [11:00AM-1:30PM]

📍 Location: [10760 Thornmint Rd, San Diego, 92127]

Looking for a day of fun, creativity, and discovery? Bring your little ones to the Flea Market for Kids—an exciting event where kids can buy, sell, trade, and explore!

🧸 What’s in Store?

• Treasure Trove of Fun: Toys, books, games, clothes, and handmade crafts!

• Kidpreneurs in Action: Support young sellers showcasing their creativity and entrepreneurial spirit.

• Interactive Activities: Face painting, and more surprises!

• Yummy Treats: Delicious Pizza and drinks to keep the fun going.

✨ Why Join?

• Teach kids about money management and recycling through trading and selling.

• Celebrate creativity, community, and fun in a safe, family-friendly environment.

• Find unique items and make lasting memories.

🌟 Free Admission!

💡 Calling All Young Vendors: Have extra toys, books, or crafts to share? Reserve a table and let your kids shine as they run their own mini-shop!
