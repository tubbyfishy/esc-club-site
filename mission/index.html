Mission Statement

Mission Statement for ESC Club:

The ESC Club, Student driving organization cooperated with the umbrella of a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization operating through Amazing Grace Institute (AGI), is dedicated to fostering multicultural communication and promoting equal opportunities in STEM education.

Comprising trilingual students proficient in English, Spanish, and Mandarin, our club embodies a strong commitment to linguistic diversity and cultural exchange. Through consistently culture-integrated programs, we actively promote STEM education within multicultural communities, with a particular focus on promoting, teaching, supporting, and advocating for underrepresented students in STEM.

Through inclusive programs, outreach initiatives, and accessible learning opportunities, we strive to bridge opportunity gaps, connect diverse communities, expand access to innovation, and cultivate the next generation of globally minded STEM leaders.

Key Components of our Mission:

Regular Exchange Program: We strive to establish a continuous flow of virtual meetings, cultural showcases, and shared projects to facilitate the exchange of knowledge and experiences.

Resource Donation: Club’s Sponsors dedicated to supporting educational initiatives, and our club commits to donating relevant resources to enhance the learning experience at partner schools & communities.

Supplementary Classes: Recognizing the significance of digital literacy, our volunteers are prepared to offer free programming , 3D printing classes to spark an interest in technology among students.

In-Person Meetings: Valuing personal interaction, we propose monthly or annual academic exchanges where our members will engage with your students, sharing experiences and participating in collaborative learning activities.

Openness to Further Projects: The ESC Club is open to exploring additional projects or initiatives that align with our mutual goals of educational and cultural exchange.

Driven by passionate and enthusiastic individuals, the ESC Club seeks to create a lasting impact on multicultural communication and STEM education. We invite collaboration and look forward to the opportunity of working together to establish a meaningful and lasting educational partnership.
