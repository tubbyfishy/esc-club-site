Frequently Asked Questions

What languages are used during class?

Languages used vary per class. For example, Spanish classes require strong skills in Spanish and English, however for Lego FLL class, only English is required.

How can I join?

You can contact us by email or fill the contact form on the contact page or using the contact information provided below.

How often are activities held?

Different programs have different frequencies. For example, Friday Night Talk occurs weekly. Classes are taught in no specific pattern.
