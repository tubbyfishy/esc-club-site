Event Details: September 27, 2025

Sponsored by top U.S. organizations and leading AI companies, this event welcomes 6th to 12th-grade students from around the world — including Taiwan, Malaysia, Singapore, Canada, Mexico, the United States, and beyond

Students will develop AI-powered applications that address real-world challenges across various industries. Through this journey, participants will gain hands-on experience, receive mentorship from AI professionals, and present their innovative projects to top industry judges. More importantly, they will learn how to use AI to uplift communities, protect our world, empower future generations, and bring hope to those in need.

Competition Tracks:

🔹 AI for Community Service
Create AI solutions that strengthen civic engagement, improve local governance, assist in disaster response, and enhance public safety.

🔹 AI for Health Services
Develop AI applications that advance medical diagnostics, mental health support, disease prevention, and access to healthcare.

🔹 AI for Environment
Design AI-driven innovations to tackle environmental challenges such as climate change, conservation, clean energy, and sustainability.

🔹 AI for Global Education
Build AI tools that make education more accessible, equitable, and personalized for learners around the world.

🔹 AI for the Next Generation
Create AI solutions that empower young people with knowledge, leadership skills, and opportunities to shape a better future.

🔹 AI for Families
Develop AI innovations that strengthen family relationships, support parenting, promote household well-being, and enrich family life.

🔹 AI for Elders and Youth
Design AI tools that promote intergenerational mentorship, learning, care, and meaningful connections between seniors and young people.

🔹 AI for the Disadvantaged
Build AI projects focused on uplifting marginalized communities providing critical resources to those most in need.
